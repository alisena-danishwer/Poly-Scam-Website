Learn About Scams

Common Scams in Malaysia

Online Shopping Scams

Fake online stores selling products at unbelievable prices that never arrive after payment.

Red Flags: Prices too good to be true, no contact information, pressure to pay quickly.

Investment Scams

Promises of high returns with little risk through fake investment platforms.

Red Flags: Guaranteed high returns, pressure to invest quickly, complex explanations.

Job Offer Scams

Fake job offers requiring payment for training or equipment that never materializes.

Red Flags: Job offers without interviews, requests for payment, vague job descriptions.

Love Scams

Romantic relationships formed online where the scammer eventually asks for money.

Red Flags: Quick declarations of love, refusal to meet in person, constant requests for money.

Tech Support Scams

Fake tech support calls or pop-ups claiming your device has a virus.

Red Flags: Unsolicited tech support, requests for remote access, demands for payment.

Bank & Government Impersonation

Scammers pretending to be from banks or government agencies to steal personal information.

Red Flags: Threats of account suspension, requests for personal details, urgency to act.
